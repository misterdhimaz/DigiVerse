Warranty Policy

Understand your product warranty coverage with DigiVerse

Our Warranty Coverage

At DigiVerse, we stand by the quality of our products. All products purchased from DigiVerse come with a limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects and hardware failures.

Warranty Period

The standard warranty period is 12 months from the date of purchase, unless otherwise specified in the product documentation.

What Is Covered

  • Hardware defects and malfunctions due to manufacturing errors
  • Faulty components replaced or repaired at no charge
  • Technical support for warranty claims

What Is Not Covered

  • Damage caused by misuse, accidents, or unauthorized modifications
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Consumable parts such as batteries, unless defective

How to Claim Warranty

  1. Contact DigiVerse Customer Support with your purchase details.
  2. Provide proof of purchase and description of the issue.
  3. Follow instructions to send your product for inspection.
  4. Receive repair, replacement, or refund based on the warranty terms.
